He was the Director of Software, for the Association of Statistics Students of Nigeria -ASSON Futo for the 2019/2020 academic session. Emmanuel was amongst the 200 out of over 36000+ participants who were shortlisted for the 2024 DSN Artificial Intelligence Physical Bootcamp that held at the University of Lagos. He participated in the In-House Project and emerged as the second runner-up for the PowerBI track. He's one of Zindi Campus ambassadors for the Federal University of Technology Owerri, and hosted a couple of events that has impacted students which includes the Free AI in Every city put together by Data Scientists Network/Data Science Nigeria in the year 2023. He was also a facilitator for the Digital Skills Africa program that held in the city of Owerri courtesy of DSN and Microsoft where he taught participants how they could build insightful dashboards using PowerBI. He has helped over 70+ person's to get started in data analysis with tools like Excel, PowerBI and Python, and wishes to raise the bar in a few years to come. He's a certified data analyst with Udacity and skilled in softwares like PowerBI, python, SQL, and he hopes to explore the data ecosystem. He has volunteered for various organizations in several which includes Hult prize Futo, Smfest, Wdp, PSMC, Futo Leadership Summit, Mara Futo, to mention but a few. Emmanuel is open to connecting with great minds that are passionate about their ecosystem.
